Robots

The TURING Lab currently has 5 robots available to students. In addition to the robts, students have access to Lego Mindstorm EV3 robot kits. CS students have the opportunity to learn more about the field of robotics through the Robotics course offered during May Term.

GROVER

The oldest robot in the Horde. A B12 from the mid-90s, equipped with 12 sonar sensors, odometry sensors, and a nice synchro-drive system. Currently, GROVER also has a Kinect sensor, a laser range finder, and a camera on-board.

Gamera

Gamera is a turtlebot from AutonmousAI. It has an ASUS RGB-D camera, a nice 192 core processor, and a Roomba-like base. It also is fully integrated into the ROS environment. A fully functional Nerf Rhino-Fire gun has been added for offensive capabilities.

Bosch

A Sony AIBO ERS7 dog.

Lego Mindstorms EV3

The lab has several Lego Mindstorms EV3 kits for students to use. These offer a good introduction to the principles of robotics, and can be pieced together and programmed to perform virtually infinite tasks.
